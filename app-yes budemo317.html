detailguy317 Messaging App (Beta Demo)

M.O.D.S. LLC DBA detailguy317 is developing a customer-service messaging app designed for auto repair shops and service advisors. This demo page exists to show the purpose and function of our messaging system during early development.

About the App

Our app will allow repair shops and service advisors to send:

  • Appointment confirmations
  • Repair status updates
  • Service reminders
  • Chat-style customer support messages
